The Lecture Room

The lecture room, room 162, is the first room that students see when entering the CTE classes at West Potomac. Many previous student's works are on display here: architectural models line the top of the cabinets, and the walls are packed with technical drawings and sketches. Here, there are around 30 desktop computers, all equipped with CAD programs such as Autodesk Inventor, AutoCAD, and Revit Architecture, and all running smoothly on Intel i7 CPUs. Mr. Franco supervises his classes from his desk at the front of the room, and holds his lectures in this room.

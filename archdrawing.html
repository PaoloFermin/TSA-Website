Architectural Drawing

This course examines the history of architecture, various construction techniques, Computer Aided Design (CAD) and residential design. Students will develop working drawings and specifications as well as build scale models. Students interested in architecture or related careers will benefit from this course. Students completing Architectural Drawing may receive three to six credits at the Northern Virginia Community College by successfully completing the Assessment by Local Examination (ABLE) administered by the NVCC staff.



A motel meant not only for cars, but for planes as well!

Students build models of buildings that not only include the buildings, but the geography surrounding them, to show how the building fits within the environment.

One of the favorite materials of architecture students is foamcore board, because it is versatile and easy to work with.

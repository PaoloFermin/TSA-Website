The Workplace: The Prototyping Room

The CTE classes at West Potomac all contain some hands-on projects and activities. Students have the opportunity to actually create something concrete, as opposed to merely discussing theory. The workshop contains many essential power tools for constructing projects, listed below:

  • Laser cutter
  • Table saw
  • Band saw
  • Scroll saw
  • Drill press
  • CNC milling machine
  • Vacuum former

Click on the images to see more!

Students are not limited to power tools: lining the far wall, there are three large cabinets full of hand tools, parts, and building materials. In addition to these cabinets, there are many scrap bins in various locations, and students often recycle old projects and turn them into something new.

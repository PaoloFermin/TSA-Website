The Workplace: The Research and Development Room

The R & D Room, Room 162, is the largest of the three rooms. Because of that, many of the competition-style projects done in the CTE classes are held in here, such as the Battlebot competition, slot car racing, and flight tests. Even with a large space in the middle, there are many tools at the student’s disposal here, including:

  • Three filament-based 3D printers
  • Two powder-based 3D printers
  • Horizontal wind tunnel
  • Vertical wind tunnel

Click on the images to see more!

Large ongoing projects are kept here, such as the flight simulator and its accompanying hydraulic pilot’s seat. In addition to current projects, there are dozens of old projects - previous TSA projects in animatronics and architecture, old class projects from years ago - from which to gather inspiration and learning from.
